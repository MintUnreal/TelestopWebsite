TELESTOP

A 3D puzzle game with a first-person view where you have to use a high-tech camera to increase, decrease the size and move objects. You will immerse yourself in the atmosphere of the portal and superliminal series of games, in the atmosphere of hopelessness and loneliness. You will have to pass tests with puzzles, pass story maps and much more!

You can also become a test creator for the map editor. You get a big sandbox to build.

The main advantages of our game:
- A large collection of maps that is replenished every day thanks to the players.
- A powerful map editor to embody any ideas in our game.
- You can also create not just puzzles, but maps with a plot and NPCs.
- The game is optimized for weak phones, while it has beautiful graphics.
- Unusual mechanics
- Interaction with the community via chat
